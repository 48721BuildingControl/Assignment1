Natural Ventilation System

General Information

In order to grant occupants more access to the nature, the design of CSL involves natural ventilation to provide fresh air with high quality. For all the regularly occupied indoor space, it is designed to have operable windows within 15 feet. It can not only provide occupants with easy access to the nature view, but also allow natural ventilation in those space to reduce energy consumption of HVAC system. The location of operable windows was designed and optimized through a computational fluid dynamics (CFD) study. The thermostat setpoint is designed to be 78°F (25.5°C) instead of conventional 72°F (25.5°C) to maximize the period allowing natural ventilation. For part period of the year, the entire building area can be adequately served by natural ventilation. Atrium space is unconditioned without mechanical heating or cooling while it implements many passive strategies such as thermal massing, phase-change material, high-performance operable glazing, solar shading to maintain temperature within comfortable level.

Below is the summary of natural ventilation features:

  • 100% access to operable windows within 15 feet

  • Thermostat setpoint raised to 78°F (25.5°C) for maximizing natural ventilation hour

  • Atrium passive design startegies:thermal massing, phase-change material, high-performance operable glazing, solar shading

Operable windows serve as one of the most significant components that enable the building to be naturally ventilated. The control of the operable windows can be achieved manually and automatically. Apart from the basic nature of the operable windows, the windows are also equipped with motor that enables controlling the opening status of the windows.

Operable Windows Control

The control logical of the operable windows is designed using software from EIKON Logic Builder. There are three modes for the window operation: OFF, AUTO and HAND. Different sensors and monitors are installed to measure the ambient environment so that different measures can be made accordingly. The measurement include:


  • Occupancy

  • Outdoor Air Temperature

  • Outdoor Air Humidity

  • Wind Speed

  • Precipitation


In addition to the sensors, monitors and motors, the windows are also equipped with a general alarm system for manual and automatic operation.
