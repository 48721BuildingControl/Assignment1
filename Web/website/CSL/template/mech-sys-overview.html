Mechanical System Overview

  • Installation: 08/15/2011 - 11/17/2011

  • Mechanical System Design: CJL Engineering

  • Combination of passive and active design

  • One variable air volume (VAV) air handling unit (AHU) with maximum supply of 12,400 CFM

  • Closed loop geothermal system installed to reduce energy load, providing preconditioned refrigerant to AHU through mechanical system on the first floor

  • Raised floor system for supply air distribution

  • Thermal radiant heating system

  • Passive design for heating and cooling unconditioned space

NATURAL VENTILATION

Natural ventilation for unconditioned space (atrium, etc.), achieving passive heating with solar collection and 100% passive cooling.

GEOTHERMAL SYSTEM

Geothermal system used to work with AHU to reduce the heating and cooling load, achiving an estimated 70% heating and cooling energy capture.

UNDERFLOOR DISTRIBUTED SYSTEM

Underfloor Distribution System (UDFS) is implemented to serve the building due to its nature of low-energy consumption and efficiency.

ENERGY-RECOVERY SYSTEM

Energy-Recovery System is served by a rooftop AHU with tri-coil design which serves the UDFS for the entire building.
